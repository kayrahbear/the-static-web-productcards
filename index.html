Jessica Doll

Like all of our dolls, Jessica is handmade from top to bottom. She is made from soft knit fabric in a light tan colorway and stuffed with wool sourced from right here in Middle Tennessee! Her curly brown hair is made from mohair locks and her dress is sewn with quilting cotton. Her shoes are crocheted with wool yarn. She has green embrodered eyes and lightly blushed cheeks. Freckles can be added at no charge. She is 18" tall and can wear American Girl clothing (or any clothing made for an 18 inch doll). All seams are double stiched to ensure she lasts through years of hugs and playtime.
